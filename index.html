The Problem

Vibe-coded ≠ production-ready

Your app runs beautifully on your machine. But ship it as-is and you're one bad actor away from disaster.

Security Holes

No auth hardening, exposed API keys, SQL injection vulnerabilities, missing rate limiting. Attackers have scripts that find these in minutes.

Downtime & Data Loss

No health checks, no backups, no redundancy. One server crash and your users — and their data — are gone.

Can't Scale

100 users? Fine. 10,000? Your single-server setup falls over. Without proper architecture, growth kills your app.

No CI/CD Pipeline

Manual deployments via SSH. No staging environment. No rollback strategy. Every push is a roll of the dice.

Zero Observability

No logging, no metrics, no alerts. When something breaks at 3am, you find out from angry users on Twitter.

Compliance Gaps

Enterprise clients need ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR evidence. Without it, big contracts stay out of reach.
